Who We Are

Powering your life with safety and reliability

We are a family run company formed and based in Flintshire. We cover all areas throughout the Northwest, North Wales, and surrounding areas. We provide a full range of domestic, commercial, and industrial electrical services to local councils, factories, schools, shops, offices, and homes.


Our reputation is exemplary with a large amount of our work coming from repeat business or recommendations from our previous customers. We offer very competitive prices, and no job is too small – just give us a call for a free non obligation quote.


We pride ourselves on offering a hassle free and affordable service where the customer’s needs always take priority, as do excellent quality workmanship and operating to modern standards. We are confident that we can meet all your requirements on your next electrical project and exceed your expectations when it comes to quality and affordable electrical contractors.


All our electricians are fully qualified to all the latest regulations and offer over 15 years’ experience in the electrical industry.
